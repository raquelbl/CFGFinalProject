Tahini Stuffed Dates

If you're like us, snacking right now is your greatest friend and greatest foe... and you're probably bored of the same old treats. So here's a new recipe we discovered which uses ingredients that are well-stocked in supermarkets, even during the pandemic. All you need is:

  • Medjool Dates (with the stone in)
  • Tahini
  • Pistachios
  • Dark Chocolate for dipping
  • Dessicated coconut

No Bake Cheesecake

It's too damn hot to turn on the oven, so here's a no bake cheesecake recipe to satisfy your sweet cravings this summer. Ingredients for the cheesecake:

  • 600g Full fat cream cheese
  • 285ml Double cream
  • 250g Digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter, melted
  • 100g Icing sugar
  • 1x Vanilla pod
Ingredients for the topping:
  • 400g Strawberries
  • 25g Icing sugar
